What is QOTD ?

QOTD is Question of the day. Each day, a question is asked; it can be serious or fun questions. To have access to the channel and get pinged whenever a question is asked, get the QOTD role in #roles

What are the dorms ?

The Dorms are where members talk of transidentity-related topics, or ask for advices on gender stuff. But they aren't exclusive; whatever you identify as, you are allowed to talk in any of the dorms ! So if you're transmasc and hesitating to post in transfem, don't worry; you can !
