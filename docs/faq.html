PluralKit and Plural people/Systems

What are systems / Plural people ?

Systems / Plural people are people who share a same body for multiple individuals, or whose identity acts as multiple beings. This can be caused by many different things; in the case of DID (Dissociative Identity Disorder), the identity of the child was unable to merge as one because of childhood trauma, unlike how it usually happen for people.

What is PluralKit ?

PluralKit is a discord bot that allows for systems and plural people to use a single discord account while still easily allowing their alters to talk without bothering to explain who is fronting every time. It uses a very smart proxy system that allows the user to switch easily between alters. The way it works can be seen in more details on the official bot website.

Why are alters listed as bots ?

Because of how Discord works, it is impossible to make alters appear as real accounts; PluralKit uses webhooks to make it possible for alters to show individual messages and allow all the features that PluralKit has. Although the different alters appear as bots, they are not bots; please do not refer to them as such unless specified differently by the alter themself.

How should I interact with these members ?

They may share their body with many other people, they are still capable of feelings and should be treated by other members as their own individuals. In some cases, alters may merge; an alter can absorb another, or two alters can fuse to become another. You are not entitled to force them into creating that alter back; you might have gotten emotionally attached to them, but just like with any other friend, you have to learn to let go when they aren't here anymore.

I think I might be plural/ a system. What should I do ?

Firstly, do not panic; this might be stressful and overwhelming to realize this, but as said earlier, it is not a fatality. Plural people and systems are nothing rare; there is roughly as much people with DID than there is red headed people in the world. One thing you will face in your journey is difficulty to have a diagnostic; your relatives and friends might downplay it and brush it off, but if you have doubts, and you are able to have an official diagnostic from a therapist specialized in this kind of issues, you might still want to try.

after obtention of a diagnostic, you will still face difficulties. Systems and plural people are widely misunderstood by the general population, and a wide range of them will hate you out of misinformation. Culture has portrayed you as a chaotic, unpredictable potentially dangerous being. Often, people might fear you will try to murder them. These are all preconceived ideas often found in media like movies, books, but also newspapers; despite the wide majority of murderers being neurotypical people, and neuro-atypical people being less likely to commit crimes than their neurotypical counterparts, they are still faced with the stigma that they are dangerous people. This is something that must be taken in consideration when telling people that you are plural; people will usually not understand, and some can react wildly.

of course, this does not mean you are doomed to loneliness; many communities like ours welcome systems and plural people and will do their best to make you feel at ease. There are probably organizations in your country who will provide help, so try to get in touch with them.

it isn't an easy life to be plural/a system, but it is not a fatality. You can find help to deal with the difficulties you will face at school, at work, and in all of your life. You can find online ressources to learn how to re-organize with your other alters in mind, and how to deal with littles or persecutors if you have any of this kind of alter.

I am a roleplayer/DND Player. Can I use PluralKit ?

even though it is specified on the official pluralKit website that it is okay to use the bot for roleplaying purpose, it is preferred by the plural community to use other bots such as tupperbox in order to do this. this is because, where the bot is only for entertainment purpose for roleplayers, it is a life saving tool for plural users, and so many people using it causes issues with data storage or malfunctions.

one could argue that plural people could be the ones to move from the bot to another; but for a bot named pluralKit, you would expect its user demographic to be, in majority, plural people, and not solely roleplayers. pluralKit was designed for plural people, and roleplayers are only tolerated to use it; but when many different tools are available, like tupperbox, we should be able to let plural people have their tools be functional, and not go offline every once in a while.
