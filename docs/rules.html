General Rules

1. Be respectful of others

Do not harass other members, or attack them ! Do not send unsettling comments or threats to other members, including chainmails or memes. Ableist, Racist, Transphobic, and other derogatory terms are banned. If you are unsure of the nature of what you're saying, do not say it.

2. Use the appropriate channels

Use the correct channels when talking in the server, especially for images.

Please vent in the venting channels; #venting for general vents, #tw-vent for potentially triggering vents, and #nsfw-vent for vents that include adult themes like sex or drugs.

3. Keep the server SFW

Keep NSFW topics in #nsfw, including references to sex or drugs.

Nude depictions of fictional characters are allowed in #nsfw to the extent of them not being erotic in any way. Pornography or erotic depictions of real or fictive beings in any way isn't allowed on the server, including #nsfw.

4. Do not sexualise minors

Discussion that in any way involves the sexualization of minors is prohibited. This includes loli, shota, CGL and ABDL.

5. Do not advertise

Do not advertise for any organisation, brand, website, community or anything resembling without our permission, except for petitions, NGO for LGBTQ+ rights, and the discord servers affiliated to us.

Additional rules

Moderation System

You will receive a warning or strike for each minor offense. If you are deemed a harm to the server, you will be removed or muted. Please ping @Mods or @Rangers for any issues you have. If you wish to remain anonymous, the ticket system in #help will only show your message to the staff members, feel free to use it !

Voice Chat Rules

Voice Chat must adhere to the server rules and guidelines. Any adult conversations should be taken to the 18-plus VC. When someone is streaming and you also wish to stream, please use a different VC. DO NOT misgender other members in chat, and do not pressure anyone who does not feel comfortable to speak.

No "mouth sounds", obnoxious noises, or talking over someone permitted. Staff will mute you and/ or give you a warning for not listening the first time.
