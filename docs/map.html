The Throne

#announcements

This is where we do all the important announcements.

Announcements can be about the server, but also announcements about other things; at the beginning of pride month, or when other kinds of events happens outside of the server, we sometimes make announcements about them.

when an annoucement is made, we ping the @pings role; this allows us to not ping the ones who would rather not be pinged for announcements.

#birthdays

This is a channel for people to be notified of other members' birthdays and send them a message to celebrate. You can setup your own birthday in #roles; be aware that the format used isn't your usual month-day format, but a day-month format.

#events

A channel to be notified of the on-going events of the server, whether it be a contest or simply playing together in VC.
